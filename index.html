About Me

2026
Graduate

I grew up in Afghanistan, a place that teaches you early that the ground can shift under your feet, and that how you respond to that defines you. I spent two years in China studying Civil Engineering on a competitive scholarship before making my way to Canada through WUSC's Student Refugee Program, which sponsored me to complete my Bachelor of Computing (Honours) at Queen's University, with a Security option.

That's a lot of countries, a lot of starting over, a lot of figuring things out from scratch. Honestly, I think it's the best training I could have gotten.

At Queen's, I gravitated toward cybersecurity not just because of the technical challenge, though the challenge is real, but because of what's at stake. Security is about protecting people. It's about the gap between what systems promise and what they actually deliver, and making sure that gap doesn't become someone's worst day. I'm actively working toward my Security+ and Network+ certifications, and I'm particularly drawn to GRC roles and the cybersecurity analyst track.

I speak six languages: English, Mandarin, Farsi, Urdu, Pashto, and Hindi. That's less of a party trick and more the result of spending my life building bridges between worlds that don't always talk to each other. That same instinct carries into how I communicate across technical and non-technical settings.

Outside of work, I love to exercise; bodybuilding keeps my body structured and my mind sharp, and I follow the security landscape closely enough to have opinions. If you're building a team that values resilience, adaptability, and someone who genuinely cares about the work, I'd love to talk.

Password Strength Analyzer

A defensive password strength checker built with Flask. Evaluates submitted passwords using entropy analysis (zxcvbn), 17 rule-based pattern checks, and optional breach-list lookups via the Have I Been Pwned API with k-anonymity. Passwords are never stored or logged. Deployed on Render with rate limiting and a full suite of HTTP security headers.

Python Flask zxcvbn HIBP API Rate Limiting Security Headers

Phishing URL Detector

A defensive phishing URL analyzer that performs static analysis across 17 detection rules covering IP hosts, brand impersonation, punycode domains, suspicious TLDs, and keyword patterns. Cross-references submissions against PhishTank, URLhaus, and Scamwave threat intelligence feeds with caching and robots.txt compliance. Never fetches URL content, eliminating SSRF risk entirely.
